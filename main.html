Architecture is the art of designing and constructing buildings that blend function with beauty. It reflects culture, history, and innovation, shaping the spaces where we live, work, and connect. From ancient temples to modern skyscrapers, architecture continuously evolves. Every building tells a story, embodying the values and aspirations of its time. The materials used—wood, stone, glass, and steel—transform raw space into something meaningful. Architecture is not just about structures; it’s about how people interact with and experience their surroundings. It balances aesthetics with practicality, creating spaces that inspire and serve the people who inhabit them. Iconic buildings often become symbols of progress, heritage, and identity. Through thoughtful design, architecture connects us to our environment, leaving lasting impressions for generations to come.

Architecture is the art and science of designing and constructing buildings and other physical structures.
